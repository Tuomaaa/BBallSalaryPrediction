2026 Free Agent Valuation Model

The NBA Free Agent Valuation Model is an open-source tool that predicts a player's market value (cap_pct) using an XGBoost regressor. Trained strictly on first-year contract data to capture genuine market signals, the model leverages 14 engineered features—including prior-season advanced metrics like DARKO DPM, LEBRON, and RAPM, alongside physical attributes and contract history. This one-year lagged approach mimics real front-office evaluations to generate objective salary projections and identify market inefficiencies.
